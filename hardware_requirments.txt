The robo was build using:

1) 1x Robot chassis Hawk01

2) 1x Arduino UNO R3(China)

3) 1x L293D dual DC Motor controller

4) 1x Directional wheel

5) 1x Battery (+ Power jack)

6) Male to male Jumper wires

7) Male to Female Jumper wires

8) 2x Yellow Rubber Tube

9) 2x DC gear motors 

10) 4x T-shaped motor support

12) 1x USB A to B cable

13) 2x IR Sensors

16) 28x Screw M3x0.5, Length 5.5mm

17) 14x Hexa spacer

18) 4x Screw M3x0.5 Length 30mm, with Nut

19) 1x on/off switch
